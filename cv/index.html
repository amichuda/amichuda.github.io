    Working Papers¶

    Labor Supply Adjustment to Adverse Agricultural Shocks: A Study of Uber and the Gig Economy\ Dissertation Topic

    • In the developing world, there is little evidence on the added value of the flexibility provided by the gig economy

    • We study the effects of shocks and as a way to see how flexibility in payment and choosing hours can aid in poverty alleviation.

    • We use a novel method of connecting drivers to their rural places of origin using a machine learning classification using driver surnames

    • We evaluate the existence of a shock based on a drought indices to find how adverse shocks affect labor supply behavior.
